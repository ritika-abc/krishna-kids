We Are Krishna Kids (Parth Enterprises)

Welcome to Krishna Kids (Parth Enterprises), where we believe in nurturing young minds with creativity, knowledge, and fun! Our company specializes in providing unique, high-quality products designed to support the all-round development of children while also offering a wide range of offerings for the entire family.

We proudly offer a variety of Made-in-India products, including ISI-marked games, puzzles, magic tricks, books, and soft toys. Our product range is inspired by the rich cultural heritage of India, particularly the stories and characters from the Ramayana, Mahabharata, and Krishna. These items are designed to not only entertain but also educate, making learning a fun and engaging experience for children of all ages.
